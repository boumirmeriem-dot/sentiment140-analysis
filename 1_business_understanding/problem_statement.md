## Problem Statement
**Problem definition**

Each tweet is someone's sentiment, either it's positive or 
negative. Each time there is a new product, a public case, or any other event, people start to publish their feelings 
about it. Therefore, teams of businesses, politicians,
and many others start to follow the trend to identify sentiment polarities.


**The question to answer**

Can we automatically identify sentiment polarities (positive or negative) from tweets' content?
